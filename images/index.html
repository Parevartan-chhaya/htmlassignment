      Heading out? Give your team a heads up.

      No matter what you’re up to or where you’re going — you can now set your status in Slack so teammates know. Choose from five default statuses for common scenarios when you’re away, or create your own (with the perfect emoji, too). To give it a try, click your name in the upper left hand corner, then select “Set a status”.

      Learn more on our blog

      Every once in a while, we like to shine a spotlight on little improvements you might’ve missed. This time around we’re highlighting a few small-yet-mighty features that keep your work moving — like the ability to find channels you’ve renamed, view your top search results, and preview GIFs in Giphy.

      Your channels, your language

      Channel names can now include a much broader set of characters and accents, so you can easily manage and organize conversations in your primary language. Whether it’s #コーヒー, #café, or #coffee, if your team likes to grab a cup together, you can start a channel just for that.

      January 18, 2017

      Threaded messaging comes to Slack

      All kinds of conversations happen in channels. Now with Threads, you can branch off and have discussions around a particular message without having to skip to a different channel or a DM. Threads move conversations to your sidebar — where you can ask and answer questions, give feedback, or go off on an inspired tangent.

      See our blog post for more on Threads, or choose your own adventure by clicking the Start a thread button on any message.

      + More Google Drive

      See that + button just there to the left of your message box? Now, if your team has Google Drive installed, this handy button packs even more punch. Click the + button to create Google Docs, Slides, and Sheets right where you’re working. You can also use it to import any file from Google Drive and share with your team in Slack, all without switching apps or juggling tabs.

      Don’t see these options, but want to use them? Just install or request Google Drive for your team, and the + menu is your oyster.
